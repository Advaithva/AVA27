Some Chemical Equations

1. CuCO3 —> CuO + CO2

When Copper Carbonate, which is green in colour, is heated strongly, it leaves behind a black residue of copper oxide and gives off carbon dioxide gas which is colourless

2. CuS + 2HCl —> CuCl2 + H2S
